About

Becky is a data journalist at Ipsos MORI in London.

She works to tell stories using Ipsos’ vast data archive on the Ipsos MORI website, on social media, and in collaboration with other journalists. As Ipsos MORI’s first data journalist, she is also building data journalism infrastructure and resources within the company.

She previously spent 18 months as a data curator at Google News Lab, and has freelanced for titles incuding The Sun, Metro.co.uk and The Guardian.
